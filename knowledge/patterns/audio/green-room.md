# Green Room Audio

A green room mix lets off-air guests hear relevant production audio before they join.

Typical routing:

- Program audio or host mic to green room bus.
- Producer talkback to green room bus only when needed.
- Guest's own mic excluded from their personal return.
- Music or playback included only if useful for cueing.

Review checks:

- Confirm which physical or virtual output carries the green room bus.
- Confirm green room audio is not routed back to master.
- Confirm comms do not leak into stream/recording.
- Confirm guests do not hear delayed copies of themselves.
